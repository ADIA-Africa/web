Chamber of Policy Studies and Development

About the Chamber

The Chamber of Policy Studies and Development is dedicated to crafting, implementing, and upgrading digital inclusion policies and standards across Africa. It fosters collaboration, awareness, and impactful strategies to ensure inclusive digital growth at all levels.

Vision & Mission

Vision

A society with policies, regulations, and standards that respond to economic, social, and political needs, fostering security, productivity, and cooperation.

Mission

To develop a resilient, supportive, and professionally managed digital policy ecosystem that empowers stakeholders and fosters regional cooperation.

Operational Highlights

  • Consolidate diverse policy frameworks across Africa to promote digital inclusion.
  • Drive policy implementation through advocacy, awareness, and strategic alignment.
  • Build capacity via training, workshops, and seminars.
  • Conduct research on digital inclusion topics aligned with policies and regulations.
  • Promote compliance and legal strategies during technological transitions.
  • Engage stakeholders across sectors and regions.
  • Encourage collaboration among African nations for a unified digital future.

Chamber Divisions

Division of Policy Research & Innovation

Develops innovative policies and conducts research to advance digital inclusion initiatives across Africa.

Division of Policy Instrumentation

Converts policies into actionable strategies and ensures their effective deployment across sectors.

Division of Training & Development

Focuses on capacity building through workshops, seminars, and training programs to enhance policy and technical skills.

Division of Digital Inclusion Legislative Affairs

Handles legislative advocacy, policy framing, and legal frameworks to support digital inclusion initiatives.

Division of Compliance & Legal Technologies

Ensures adherence to policies, standards, and legal frameworks, promoting responsible deployment of digital technologies.
