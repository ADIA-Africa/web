Regional Digital Inclusion Councils

Comprising representatives from all five Chambers of the Africa Digital Inclusion Alliance—Skill Development, Device Access, Technical Support, Marginalized Groups Inclusion, and Broadband & Policy—these councils operate regionally to bridge the digital divide and foster collaboration with economic communities, trade blocs, and corporate partners across Africa’s seven regions.

About the Regional Digital Inclusion Councils

These councils bring together representatives from all five chambers of ADIA, working regionally to accelerate digital inclusion efforts. Collaborating with regional economic communities, trade blocs, and corporate partners, they aim to bridge gaps, promote regional strategies, and foster sustainable digital growth across Africa’s seven regions.
