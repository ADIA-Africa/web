About the Network

The Africa Digital Inclusion Diaspora Network is a vibrant community of Africans outside the continent committed to fostering positive digital development in Africa. Driven by the Africa Digital Inclusion Alliance, this network spans five continents—Europe, Asia, North America, South America, and Australia—uniting efforts to contribute to Africa’s digital growth.

Headed by continental directors, the network leads initiatives that promote collaboration, innovation, and sustainable digital transformation across Africa.

Core Pillars

Clear Coordination

Structured leadership and organized efforts to ensure impactful contributions across all units.

Policy Interventions

Advocating for policies that support digital growth and inclusive innovation in Africa.

Agile Driven Conversations

Facilitating dynamic dialogues that accelerate digital solutions and collaborations.

Network Units & Events

  • Units & Departments: Specialized groups focusing on research, advocacy, technology, and community engagement.
  • Events: Conferences, webinars, and workshops promoting digital inclusion and diaspora contributions.
