Our Centers of Excellence

Dedicated to advancing digital equity, our Centers of Excellence act as strategic engines for local industry development, fostering innovation, and ensuring seamless operations across community networks. We have seven specialized centers committed to transforming Africa’s digital landscape.

1. Africa Digital Inclusion Media

Driving digital storytelling, content creation, and media innovation to amplify Africa’s digital voice.

2. Africa Digital Inclusion Social Center

Fostering social innovation, community engagement, and inclusive digital participation across nations.

3. Commission for Independent Monitoring & Development

Ensuring transparent oversight and continuous progress in digital development initiatives.

4. Super Digital Innovation Unit

Pioneering cutting-edge digital innovations and fostering startup ecosystems.

5. Africa Digital Hubs & Centers Network

Connecting regional hubs to promote collaboration, resource sharing, and scaling of digital solutions.

6. Institute of Digital Equity Professional Studies

Training and certifying professionals to lead digital transformation initiatives.

7. Center for Emerging Tech Governance

Guiding policies and frameworks for emerging technologies such as AI, IoT, and blockchain.
