About ADIGA

ADIGA (Africa Digital Inclusion General Assembly) is the flagship annual event that brings together the leadership and affiliates of the Africa Digital Inclusion Alliance. Held at the beginning of each year, it gathers representatives from executive councils, governmental bodies, corporate partners, and project implementers to discuss the state of digital inclusion, challenges, needs, and strategic preparations to promote digital equity across Africa.

Hosted by the President of the General Assembly and the Chief Executive Officer of the Alliance's Executive Council, this platform serves as the official forum for fostering collaboration, aligning operations, adopting strategies, and advocating for legislative development to advance digital inclusion continent-wide.
