About the Africa Specialized Multi-Sectoral Digital Council

This council serves as a high-level advisory and strategic body, bringing together experts from diverse sectors, government officials, academia, NGOs, advocacy groups, and international organizations. Its mission is to map out and develop key areas of digital inclusion across Africa, ensuring a coordinated approach that accelerates sustainable growth and innovation.
