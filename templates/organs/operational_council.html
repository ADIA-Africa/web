About the National Operational Councils

These councils serve as vital links between the Africa Digital Inclusion Alliance’s operational chambers and the key bodies responsible for ICT development, regulation, and policy-making within African nations. They foster collaboration with ministries, local authorities, and industry regulators to advance digital inclusion at the national level.
