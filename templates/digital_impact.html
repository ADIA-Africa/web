Multi-sectoral Digital Impact

About the Digital Impact Initiative

The Multi-sectoral Digital Impact initiative aims to foster innovation and technological transformation across various industries. We work collaboratively to develop impactful solutions that drive sustainable growth, enhance connectivity, and empower communities through digital strategies.

Our goal is to bridge gaps in digital access, promote cross-sector collaboration, and create an ecosystem where ideas flourish for societal benefit.

Vision

"A society where everyone has access to affordable, meaningful digital tools to enhance productivity and well-being."

Mission

"To democratize access, use, and ownership of digital devices for everyone, irrespective of conditional ties, fostering better livelihoods."

Core Operations

  • Promoting affordability, accessibility, ownership, and safe use of digital devices such as computers.
  • Setting up, maintaining, and upgrading from simple computer labs to STEM labs, then to SMART labs for primary education.
  • Building, supporting, and evolving labs for high schools and middle-level schools to STEAM and Smart labs.
  • Collaboratively managing a Digital Inclusion Research Laboratory within tertiary institutions for academic and societal impact.
  • Supporting local assembling of computers across Africa.
  • Researching, monitoring, and evaluating the value chain of digital device development in Africa.
  • Supporting democratization of digital device accessories through outreach programs.
  • Aligning community needs via a device access bank with comprehensive information support.

Chamber Divisions

Division of Partnership for Device Access

Focuses on building strategic partnerships to improve device access across communities.

Division of Device Ownership Assistance

Provides support programs to facilitate device ownership in underserved areas.

Division of Community Device Access

Ensures community-based access points and digital literacy programs.

Division of Africa Computer Assembly

Promotes local manufacturing and assembly of computers across Africa.

Division of Africa Devices Manufacturing

Supports the development of local manufacturing capacities for digital devices.
