Chamber of Technical Support Management

About Chamber

The Chamber of Technical Support Management is secondarily driven and mandated to ensure efficient, productive, resilient, effective and relevant adoption and adaptation to forms of technology in both skill development, device access and other digital inclusion work. The Chamber focuses on deeper analysis, technical needs and support profiling including various technical resources, needs, differences and direct operations to sustain digital inclusion work.

Vision

"A digitally sustained, productive, resilient, effective and reliable technical access frameworks for a well-managed digital inclusion ecosystem."

Mission

To create a resilient, supportive and professionally managed technical support ecosystem for people, institutions, and communities for productive utilization of technological resources.

General Operations

  • Promoting digital skills, device use and efficient management processes by monitoring and sustaining key digital inclusion operations.
  • Provision of reliable and quality technical support by offering software, hardware and external solutions.
  • Operating to drive a resilient technical support and management ecosystem for organizations through partnerships and alignment.
  • Driving E-waste collection, management, and planning across sectors and regions in Africa.
  • Offering key technology-driven consultative support to individuals and organizations.
  • Supporting software transitions, updates, industry standardization, and general operations.

Chamber Divisions

Division of Academia Technical Management

Focuses on managing academic technical resources, supporting educational institutions in adopting digital tools, and fostering academic collaborations.

Division of Corporate Technical Management

Handles corporate technical support, ensuring organizations optimize their technological resources and infrastructure for maximum productivity.

Division of Community Technical Service

Supports community-based technical initiatives, local digital literacy, and access programs to empower underserved populations.

Division of E-waste Management

Focuses on sustainable e-waste collection, recycling, and management strategies aligned with eco-friendly practices.

Division of Technology Transformation

Drives innovation, industry upgrades, and digital transformation initiatives for better adaptation and competitiveness.
