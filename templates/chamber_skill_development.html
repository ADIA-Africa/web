Chamber of Skill Development

About

The Chamber of Skill Development is foundational to digital inclusion work for Africa Digital Inclusion Alliance. It administers lifelong digital learning pathways through skills development, upskilling, reskilling, and cross-skilling for education, work, and enterprise. Our focus is on curriculum-based training, capacity building, and managing trainees to ensure they can productively use their skills within relevant frameworks tailored to diverse groups across educational institutions and businesses.

Vision

A society where individuals are digitally aware, competent, and empowered through lifelong learning pathways fostering productivity and societal growth.

Mission

To embed a lifelong digital learning mindset for personal and societal advancement, ensuring inclusive access and skill development.

Operations

  • Deliver foundational to intermediate digital literacy skills for primary students.
  • Empower the workforce through capacity building and resilience training.
  • Support businesses with digital skills for efficiency and growth.
  • Integrate digital inclusion programs into tertiary education to sustain relevance.
  • Foster digital innovation and skills development in high schools and middle schools.

Divisions

Primary Digital Inclusion

Promotes digital skills for primary schools, nurturing soft and hard skills to foster innovation, literacy, and STEM readiness.

Post-Primary Digital Skills

Focuses on high schools and middle schools through curriculum training, innovation hubs, and digital clubs to nurture future-ready learners.

Tertiary Digital Outreach

Extends digital inclusion efforts to universities and colleges through leadership workshops, monitoring, and community impact initiatives.

Corporate Digital Resilience

Develops frameworks for organizational productivity, digital transformation, and capacity building via partnerships and corporate training.

Enterprise Digital Growth

Supports SMEs with skills development, digital tools, mentorship, and long-term growth opportunities.
