CHAMBER OF MARGINALIZED GROUPS INCLUSION

ABOUT THE CHAMBER

Chamber of Marginalized Groups Inclusion acts to drive social inclusion through digital inclusion by targeting the diverse nature of populations in society. It enables the ability to learn, communicate, work, effectively socialize, and be productive irrespective of any conditional ties.

It caters to populations less considered in adopting and growing with various forms of technology in various contexts of needs and aspirations at individual or community levels. This helps overcome systemic, structural, historical, or cultural barriers within governance, societal norms, or for marginalized groups.

The chamber also aims to instill a sense of human support, self-reliance, and connectedness, reducing harmful addictive behaviors related to technology and promoting mental, emotional, and psychological growth.

CHAMBER'S VISION AND MISSION

VISION

"A society where technology meets everyone where they are, when and how they need it towards a productive and healthy relational society."

MISSION

"To build digital inclusion as a culture that thinks, considers, grows and empowers the marginalized communities for a safer society."

GENERAL OPERATIONS

  • Awareness & Innovation: Promoting digital security, productivity, and awareness among marginalized communities.
  • Inclusive Culture: Leaving no one behind, regardless of psychological, mental, physical, or regional barriers.
  • Disability Inclusion: Promoting digital solutions for various disabilities.
  • Correctional Support: Empowering incarcerated individuals through digital programs.
  • Digital Poverty: Overcoming digital poverty in rural and underserved areas.
  • Development in Arid Lands: Promoting digital growth in Arid and Semi-Arid Lands.
  • Human Services: Managing programs for digital care, protection, and support.
