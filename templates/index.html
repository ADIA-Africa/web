Our Mission

Africa Digital Inclusion Alliance is committed to creating inclusive digital ecosystems across the continent, ensuring that technology serves as a catalyst for sustainable development and social equity.

Connectivity

Establishing internet infrastructure and connectivity solutions in underserved communities to bridge the digital divide.

Education

Providing digital literacy training and technology education programs to empower individuals with essential 21st-century skills.

Empowerment

Creating opportunities for economic growth through digital entrepreneurship, e-commerce, and online job creation.

Our Key Organs

Africa Digital Inclusion Alliance is composed of seven organs.

Africa Digital Inclusion Diaspora Network

A network of Africans outside the continent contributing to digital development across 5 continents, with pillars like policy, knowledge exchange, and philanthropy.

Operates quarterly with an annual engagement forum.

Policy and Advocacy

Focuses on influencing digital policies, regulations, and standards to foster inclusive growth.

Engages stakeholders at all levels to ensure sustainable digital development.

Knowledge and Innovation

Promotes sharing of best practices, research, and innovative projects to accelerate digital inclusion.

Hosts forums, webinars, and training programs.
