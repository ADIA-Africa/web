About Us

We are a pan-African driven ecosystem organization seeking to bridge the digital divide through inclusive frameworks addressing skills, internet & device access, policy research, advocacy, and marginalized groups' inclusion. Our partnership-centric approach drives digital growth at community and continental levels.

  • Learning Institutions & Partners: Universities, colleges, vocational institutions, and schools providing digital skills training, capacity building, and research collaborations.
  • Corporate Partners: Engaged in digital capacity building, resilience programs, and services.
  • Governments: Policy formulation, digital inclusion events, and advocacy.
