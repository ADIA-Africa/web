CHAMBER OF DEVICE ACCESS AND DEVELOPMENT

About the Chamber

The Chamber of Device Access and Development is a vital part of digital inclusion efforts. It promotes, provides, and sustains meaningful access to digital devices, supporting learning institutions, businesses, and society at large. Our goal is to make digital devices affordable and accessible, fostering positive learning outcomes and productive work through strategic partnerships and innovative frameworks.

The Chamber operates by enhancing affordability and accessibility of digital devices such as laptops, computers, and key accessories. We focus on strategies that prioritize positive educational outcomes and productive work, supported by well-defined financial partnerships and frameworks.

Vision

"A digitally inclusive society through meaningful, affordable, and accessible digital devices to enhance productivity, fulfillment, and positive life outcomes."

Mission

"To democratize access, use, and ownership of digital devices for everyone, irrespective of conditional ties, fostering better livelihoods."

Core Operations

  • Promoting affordability, accessibility, ownership, and safe use of digital devices such as computers.
  • Setting up, maintaining, and upgrading from simple computer labs to STEM labs, then to SMART labs for primary education.
  • Building, supporting, and evolving labs for high schools and middle-level schools into STEAM and Smart labs.
  • Building, maintaining, and collaboratively managing a Digital Inclusion Research Laboratory within tertiary institutions for academic and societal impact.
  • Promoting local assembling of computers across Africa.
  • Researching, monitoring, and evaluating the digital device value chain in Africa.
  • Supporting democratization of digital device accessories through outreach programs.
  • Aligning community needs via a device access bank with comprehensive information support.

Chamber Divisions

Division of Partnership for Device Access

Focuses on building strategic partnerships to improve device access across communities.

Division of Device Ownership Assistance

Provides support programs to facilitate device ownership in underserved areas.

Division of Community Device Access

Ensures community-based access points and digital literacy programs.

Division of Africa Computer Assembly

Promotes local manufacturing and assembly of computers across Africa.

Division of Africa Devices Manufacturing

Supports the development of local manufacturing capacities for digital devices.
